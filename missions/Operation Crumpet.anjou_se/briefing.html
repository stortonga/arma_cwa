With several covert operations underway in order to disrupt Nazi Germany's retreat and its supply lines along the Western Front in the wake of Operation Overlord, a number of small detatchments of the British Army's 1st Airborne Division are about to jump deep behind enemy lines.

Your section is tasked to eliminate General Reinhard Greif, the Nazi's head of SS operations in the north western region of France. Greif is known for his very calculating character and feared for his sadistic behaviour towards civilians. Putting an end to him and his tyranny will weaken the enemy's chain of command in France as well.

Your orders are:

Jump into your dropzone.

Make contact with the informant.

Neutralize Reinhard Greif.

Reach the escape point.

This mission dossier will provide you and your team with all information regarding your operation. The dossier is divided into the following sections:

Insertion


Our American allies have agreed to provide the means of transportation on your ingress route to the target area. A C-47 will therefore take off from the frontline airstrip north of Meaux by 1700Z and take you to the dropzone marked on the map handed out to you earlier today.
In addition to the very members of your team, a weapon cache in the form of a drop canister is to be delivered as well. The canister will contain additional weapons and explosives, should you need any in order to achieve your objectives.

Contacts


Over the last months, a SOE agent has been operating in the area to gather information about enemy positions, activities and habits that would facilitate subversive actions from our side. Your team is scheduled to rendezvous with the informant not later than [1730Z] at a small shed located within map quadrant [Ec85]. Approach the site with utmost caution in order not to compromise the informant's position. With a strict curfiew in place between [1600Z] and [0600Z] for all civilians, he will be risking his life just by being outside, let alone making contact with you.

The informant will provide your team with then current information regarding your target's location and possible enemy positions that he is aware of at the time. Make sure to take these into consideration while planning your next actions in the field.
